The Relationship Project is a space for people to be inspired and grow through sharing and celebrating real life stories, and learning the ‘why’ behind what makes people’s relationships work for them!

Ultimately we want to inspire people to have meaningful, authentic, fulfilling relationships.

On our weekly podcast we’ll be chatting with people about their stories, celebrating their awesomeness, and exploring with relevant experts why their relationship is thriving and what we can learn from them.
